Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started

The frequency depends on your goals, budget, and schedule. Most clients see best results with 2-3 sessions per week combined with independent workouts. Beginners often benefit from more frequent sessions initially to establish proper form and routine.

For your first session, wear comfortable workout clothes and athletic shoes. Bring a water bottle and towel. We'll provide all necessary equipment. Your trainer may ask you to complete some health forms beforehand.

Yes! All our trainers can provide basic nutrition guidance. For comprehensive meal planning and macro tracking, we offer specialized nutrition coaching packages. Proper nutrition is essential for achieving optimal results from your training.

We require 24 hours notice for session cancellations to avoid being charged. For emergencies or illness, please notify us as soon as possible. We understand life happens and will work with you on a case-by-case basis.

Most clients report feeling stronger and more energetic within 2-4 weeks. Visible changes typically appear in 6-8 weeks with consistent training and nutrition. Remember, sustainable transformation takes time - we focus on long-term health, not quick fixes.
